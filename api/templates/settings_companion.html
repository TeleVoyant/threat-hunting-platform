Notification settings

1. Install on your phone

Sideload the signed Android .apk onto your phone. Open Settings → Apps → Install unknown apps once if your phone hasn't allowed sideloads.

2. Scan to enrol

Open the app → tap Scan QR → point at this code. The token is single-use and expires in 10 minutes.

After enrolment

  • The app shows a persistent "APT THP — listening for detections" notification while the listener service is running. Don't swipe it away.
  • Every detection that meets your minimum-severity threshold (configured in Notification settings) lands as a native phone notification.
  • Tap Acknowledge to ack the alert directly from the lock screen, or Open to read details in the app.
  • Enrolment rotates your API key. Your dashboard session keeps working (it uses a separate cookie), but if you had the dashboard API key copied elsewhere, that copy is now invalid.
